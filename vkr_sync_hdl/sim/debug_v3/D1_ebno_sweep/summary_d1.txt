Observations
------------
1. Loss budget (MER_ideal − MER_Verilog) monotonically DECREASES
   from 19.8 dB at EbN0=60 down to ~1.5 dB at EbN0=20.
   - At high SNR the loss is dominated by Gardner self-noise + Q1.15
     quantization (constant ≈ 20 dB ceiling above ideal floor).
   - At low SNR thermal noise dominates and Verilog tracks ideal
     within ~2 dB.

2. μ_lock drifts up with falling EbN0 (0.700 → 0.775 between 60 and
   20 dB) — smooth shift of Gardner equilibrium under self-noise,
   not a defect. μ_std grows ×4 (62 → 257).

3. At EbN0 = 15 dB the loop is below the lock threshold:
   - μ_std = 1182, vi_reg = −503 (vs. −14 at EbN0=20),
   - debug_w drifts away from 32768, mean μ collapses to ~0.5,
   - MER_Verilog = 6.2 dB even though ideal = 9.3 dB
     (3 dB cycle-slip penalty).
   Lock threshold for this architecture lies between 15 and 20 dB
   at τ=0.3.

4. Original ТЗ target «MER ≥ 18 dB at EbN0=20» is not reachable with
   parabolic Farrow α=0.5 + Gardner TED + B_n·T=0.02: ideal sync
   already gives only 14.1 dB at this point (RRC truncation + AWGN).
   Updated realistic target should be «MER_Verilog ≥ MER_ideal − 2 dB
   at EbN0 ≥ 20 dB», which IS achieved (1.48 dB loss).
